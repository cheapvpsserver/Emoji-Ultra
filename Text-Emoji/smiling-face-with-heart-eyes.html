Emoji Meaning & Appearance

The Smiling Face with Heart-Eyes emoji 😍 is a smiling face with hearts for eyes, representing intense love, adoration, or infatuation. It is often used to express strong romantic feelings or deep affection for someone or something.

Appearance: A yellow face with blushing cheeks, a closed-mouth smile, and two pink or red hearts replacing the eyes. The hearts can vary in size and position across different platforms, but always convey the same message of love.

Usage: Used to express passionate love, admiration, or attraction. Commonly used in romantic contexts, to show appreciation for someone's appearance, or to express love for food, animals, or other things that bring joy. Can also be used to convey crush-like feelings or to flirt playfully.
