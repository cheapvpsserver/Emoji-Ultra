Emoji Meaning & Appearance

The Smiling Face with Hearts emoji ­ЪЦ░ is a smiling face surrounded by floating hearts, representing overwhelming love, affection, or adoration. It is often used to express deep, warm feelings for someone or something.

Appearance: A yellow face with closed eyes, a gentle smile, and several pink or red hearts floating around it. The hearts can vary in number and size across different platforms, but always convey the same message of love and warmth.

Usage: Used to express deep affection, love, or appreciation. Commonly used in romantic contexts, to show love for family and friends, or to express adoration for cute things, animals, or experiences that bring joy. Can also be used to convey a sense of being loved or cherished.
