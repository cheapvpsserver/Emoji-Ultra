Emoji Meaning & Appearance

The Rolling on the Floor Laughing emoji 🤣 is a popular emoji used to express uncontrollable laughter. It is often used when something is extremely funny.

Appearance: A yellow face with a wide grin, tears of joy streaming from its eyes, and a body that appears to be rolling on the floor from laughter. It conveys intense amusement and hilarity.

Usage: Used to express extreme laughter, amusement, or when something is so funny it makes you roll on the floor laughing. Often used in response to jokes, funny videos, or humorous situations in casual conversations and social media.
