Emoji Meaning & Appearance

The Face with Tears of Joy emoji 😂 is one of the most popular emojis used to express extreme laughter. It is often used when something is so funny that it brings tears to the eyes.

Appearance: A yellow face with a wide, open-mouthed smile, closed eyes, and tears of joy streaming down its cheeks. It conveys intense amusement and hilarity.

Usage: Used to express uncontrollable laughter, amusement, or when something is extremely funny. Often used in response to jokes, funny videos, or humorous situations in casual conversations and social media. It's also commonly used to lighten the mood or show appreciation for something funny.
