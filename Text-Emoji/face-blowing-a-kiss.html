Emoji Meaning & Appearance

The Face Blowing a Kiss emoji 😘 is a smiling face with a kissing mouth and a heart, representing affection, love, or a kiss. It is often used to express love, gratitude, or to send a virtual kiss to someone.

Appearance: A yellow face with blushing cheeks, a winking eye, a kissing mouth, and a small heart floating near the mouth. The heart can vary in color and size across different platforms, but always conveys the same message of love.

Usage: Used to express love, affection, or to send a virtual kiss. Commonly used in romantic contexts, to show appreciation, or to express fondness for someone or something. Can also be used to flirt playfully or to say goodbye with affection.
