The Smiling Face with Halo emoji üòá is a friendly face with a small halo above its head, representing innocence, goodness, or angelic behavior. It is often used to express sweetness, purity, or to joke about being well-behaved.

Appearance: A yellow face with closed eyes, a gentle smile, and a small, golden halo floating above its head. The halo is typically depicted as a simple circle or ring, symbolizing holiness or innocence.

Usage: Used to convey innocence, kindness, or virtuous behavior. Can be used sincerely to express goodness or ironically to poke fun at someone pretending to be innocent. Often used in contexts related to children, pets, or acts of kindness.
