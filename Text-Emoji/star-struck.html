The Star-Struck emoji 🤩 is a smiling face with star-shaped eyes, representing excitement, admiration, or being impressed by someone or something famous or amazing. It is often used to express awe or fascination.

Appearance: A yellow face with blushing cheeks, a wide smile, and two star-shaped eyes. The stars can vary in style across different platforms, but always convey the same message of excitement and admiration.

Usage: Used to express being star-struck, impressed, or excited about meeting someone famous, seeing something amazing, or experiencing something extraordinary. Commonly used in contexts related to celebrities, achievements, or impressive events.
